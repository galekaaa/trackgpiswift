Frequently Asked Questions

What is a UETR code?

A Unique End-to-End Transaction Reference used to trace SWIFT payments.

How long does it take to trace a payment?

In most cases, analysis is done within minutes, but complex cases may take longer.

Can I track payments without a UETR?

No, UETR is required to identify and analyze the transaction.
